"Yellow-Ringed Sea Slug"

Janolus flavoanulatus

The yellow-ringed sea slug resides in the shallow, subtidal zones

with frequently fluctuating water temperatures. Their

diet consists of moss animals, like , and on the

ocean floor. The color of the food they eat influences the vibrancy

of the color on their bodies. Unfortunately, habitat loss and pollution

pose great threats to these nudibranchs.
